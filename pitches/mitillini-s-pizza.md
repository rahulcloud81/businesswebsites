# Mitillini's Pizza Website Pitch

## Positioning

**NW pizza and steakhouse with old-school Calgary pizza, Greek-leaning specials, and thick topping comfort.** The proposed homepage should feel unmistakably specific to Mitillini's Pizza: a pizza-box microsite: folded slices, greek tile borders, and half-price sunday callouts.

## Why this direction fits

Official/delivery menus show pizza, pasta, sandwiches, broiler items, Mitillinis Special, El Greco, spinach and feta, and phone-order usefulness.

Reference used for brand cues: https://www.mitillinispizza.ca/

## Homepage value without extra operations

- Gives customers one owned, trustworthy place to confirm what the business offers before they call, visit, book, or use an existing delivery platform.
- Uses readable text for menus, services, hours, location, and specialties so customers do not have to pinch-zoom image menus or scroll old social posts.
- Improves local SEO by giving Google clear words around specialties such as Mitillinis Special, El Greco pizza, Spinach & feta.
- Preserves the business’s current workflow by linking out to existing booking, delivery, phone, or map tools instead of adding a custom checkout or order flow.
- Creates a stronger sales story: the business looks more established, premium, and searchable while staff keep doing exactly what already works.

## Recommended first version

Launch the homepage as a lightweight demo with brand-forward visuals, four featured menu/service cards, reviews or proof points, hours, map, and contact buttons. Expand later only if the owner wants dedicated pages for catering, galleries, events, or team profiles.
